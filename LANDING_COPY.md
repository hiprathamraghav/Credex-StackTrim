## FAQ

**Do I need to log in?** No. The audit runs before any email capture.

**Why do you ask for email later?** The email gate appears only after the result so users get value first.

**Is the public report private?** The public page strips email, company, and role.

**Is AI doing the math?** No. AI writes the summary; deterministic rules calculate savings.

**When does Credex appear?** Credex is shown prominently when the audit finds more than $500/month in potential savings.
